Metastatic tumors evolve into heterogeneous cellular ecosystems, making treatment response variable across lesions and patients.

## Research Scope

My work focuses on molecular heterogeneity, metastatic evolution, and treatment-response modeling using patient-derived systems.

## Active Directions

### Molecular Tumor Heterogeneity in Metastatic Cancers

I study how competing subclones emerge and persist under selective pressure, creating resistant compartments that may drive relapse.

### Metastatic Evolution and Dissemination Dynamics

I investigate how early and late clones seed distant organs and how transcriptomic patterns reflect metastatic trajectories.

### Patient-Derived Organoids and Biomarker Discovery

I use patient-derived tumor organoids (PDOs) to profile drug sensitivity and identify biomarkers of response and resistance.
